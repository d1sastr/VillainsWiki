About Alison

A young, graceful member of the Crown. When between her duties, she works as a dancer in a prestigious ballet troupe. Her dance dazzles everyone while she masks the pain. If only life was as beautiful as her dance...

Appearance

Alison is a young woman with green eyes and black hair that is often tied back in a bun. She has a mole on the right side of her nose.

Personality

Alison is cautious, reserved, and perhaps a bit secretive. She seems to have an underlying hesitation or mistrust towards others, likely as a result of her past experiences. Despite this, she is able to interact with people in a generally amicable manner, suggesting that she has learned how to adapt and present a sociable facade. Those who are persistent enough to get to know her better may discover that she can be quite enjoyable and engaging to interact with, suggesting a brighter and more open-minded side beneath her exterior caution.
